# Prompt for Historical Graph

Below is an AI prompt that could be used to generate a graph of historical date ranges and events:

---

Create a historical graph that ranges range from Jan 1, 1910 until Jan 1, 2025.  Add green colored date ranges for World War 1, World War 2, the Korean war, and the Vietnam War, and add blue colored date ranges for economic recessions after Jan 1, 1960.  Add yellow date ranges that depict the time ranges of NASA's Gemini, Apollo and Space Shuttle programs.  For events include 1) the founding of the Federal Reserve, 2) the start of Charles Limbaugh's flight, 3) the first landing on the moon, 4) the Challenger explosion and 5) the fall of the Berlin wall.  Have the event labels include both the title and date of the event.
